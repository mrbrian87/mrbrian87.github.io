Brian Storey

Bio

My name is Brian. I was born in Etobicoke and lived in Bolton until I was 4 years old. My family then moved to Burlington and I have lived there ever since.

I am married to a wonderful woman named Melissa, and together we have 3 children. Our oldest is 4 and is named Theo, and we have twin 2 year olds: Samantha, and Joshua.

Work History

My first job I ever had was as a "rink rat" or maintenance at Skyway arena in Burlington. My duties were mostly just to keep everything clean, but I also did get the opportunity to drive the Zamboni to clear the ice.

After I left there I worked doing data entry for a medical billing company. I worked there until an opportunity came along to join a travelling carnival.

Hobbies and Interests

My main hobby is that I play bass guitar. I started playing when I was 14 and have always considered it to be a core part of who I am. In my youth I played in some bands that wrote original music, but mostly now I play in cover bands with friends. We mainly cover rock and alt-rock music from the 90s and early 2000s. I also have an interest in video games, trivia, and learning new things. For example, I am having a blast learning different Javascript and CSS tricks while building this page!
